Image Guidelines

  • Size: 1280x800 pixels. Avoid using larger images! The Raspberry Pi isn't that powerful; large images may cause some slowdown.
  • Formats: .png, .gif, .jpg, .jpeg.

Updating/Deleting Images

  • You will be a given a URL allowing you to delete your image. Please store this somewhere safe as you cannot get it again.
  • To update, delete the old image first and re-upload (otherwise your image will be duplicated)
